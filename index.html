About Pepfound

Our Mission

Pepfound exists to provide accurate, independent, and medically-reviewed information about peptides. We believe scientific knowledge should be accessible to everyone, free from corporate bias and marketing hype.

Who Reviews Content

All peptide guides are reviewed by medical professionals with expertise in peptide science, endocrinology, and clinical research. Our reviewers ensure information is accurate, evidence-based, and presented fairly.

How We Source

Our guides are built on peer-reviewed clinical studies, published research, and expert consensus. Every claim is supported by scientific evidence. We transparently cite our sources.

Our Values

  • Independence — No financial ties to manufacturers.
  • Accuracy — Scientific integrity, always first.
  • Transparency — We disclose sources and methodology openly.
  • Education — We empower informed decision-making.

Safety & Disclaimers

Important: This information is educational only. It does not replace professional medical advice.

Before Using Any Peptide

  • Consult a qualified healthcare provider
  • Discuss potential risks specific to your health
  • Peptides are not regulated like pharmaceuticals in most countries
  • Be aware of quality and purity concerns with non-pharmaceutical sources

Regulatory Status

The regulatory status of peptides varies by country. In the US, most research peptides are not FDA-approved for human use and may only be sold as research chemicals. Always verify the legal status in your region.

Medical Disclaimer

Pepfound and its contributors are not medical doctors and do not provide medical advice. The information provided is for educational purposes only. Nothing on this site should be construed as a recommendation or substitute for professional medical consultation.

Users assume full responsibility for their decisions regarding peptide use.
