About Pepfound

Safety & Medical Disclaimer

Important: This information is for educational purposes only and should not replace professional medical advice.

General Safety Information

Peptides are powerful biological molecules that affect the body's systems. Before using any peptide:

  • Consult with a qualified healthcare provider
  • Discuss potential risks and benefits specific to your health
  • Understand that peptides are not regulated like pharmaceuticals in many countries
  • Be aware of quality and purity concerns with non-pharmaceutical sources

Regulatory Status

The regulatory status of peptides varies significantly by country and jurisdiction. In the United States, most research peptides are not FDA-approved for human use and may only be sold as research chemicals. Always verify the legal status in your region before purchasing or using any peptide.
