AOD 9604: Complete Guide to Fat Loss & Metabolism

AOD 9604 is a modified fragment of human growth hormone (amino acids 176-191) engineered specifically to promote lipolysis (fat loss) while avoiding metabolic side effects. Unlike full-length GH, AOD 9604 targets adipose tissue without affecting insulin levels or promoting glycogenolysis.

This guide covers AOD 9604's mechanism, fat loss benefits, metabolic effects, dosing, and clinical research.

Key Points

  • AOD 9604 promotes fat loss without affecting insulin or glucose
  • Targets visceral and subcutaneous fat mobilization
  • Does not suppress appetite or cause metabolic side effects
  • Typical dosing: 300-600 mcg per day
  • Usually injected subcutaneously
  • Excellent safety profile
  • Works best combined with diet and exercise

What is AOD 9604?

AOD 9604 is a synthetic peptide consisting of 15 amino acids that represent the lipolytic fragment of human growth hormone. This fragment was engineered to promote fat burning without the metabolic side effects associated with full-length GH supplementation.

Mechanism of Action

AOD 9604 binds to GH receptors on adipocytes (fat cells), activating Gi-protein coupled pathways that increase cAMP and promote triglyceride hydrolysis. It mobilizes fat from adipose tissue through lipolysis while maintaining normal insulin function and glucose metabolism.

Fat Loss Benefits

  • Direct adipose tissue mobilization
  • Visceral fat reduction (most metabolically dangerous fat)
  • Subcutaneous fat loss
  • Improved body composition with diet/exercise
  • No appetite suppression or metabolic disturbance

Dosing

  • Typical dose: 300-600 mcg per day (one or two injections)
  • Frequency: Daily or 5-6 days per week
  • Duration: 8-12 week cycles
  • Timing: Pre-workout or morning optimal

Administration

Subcutaneous injection, 25-30 gauge needle, abdomen or thigh, easy self-administration, minimal injection discomfort.

Safety

Excellent safety profile. No serious adverse events. Does not suppress insulin or cause hyperglycemia. Well-tolerated at therapeutic doses. Minimal side effects.

Research: Clinical studies demonstrate AOD 9604's efficacy for fat loss without metabolic side effects. Does not affect insulin sensitivity or blood glucose.

FAQ

How fast does it work? Fat loss noticeable within 4-8 weeks with proper diet/exercise. Does it work without exercise? More effective with exercise and caloric deficit. Safe long-term? Yes, can be used in cycles safely.
